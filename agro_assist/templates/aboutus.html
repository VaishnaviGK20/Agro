About Agro Assist

Agro Assist is a farmer-to-customer grocery platform built to reduce middlemen and deliver fresh produce at fair prices.

Farmers can list products with images, quantity, and price. Customers can browse, search, add items to cart, and checkout with cash on delivery or online payment.

We use email OTP verification for secure registration and password reset.
